Pipes

Pipes are used to transform variables inside of a Component's template by following the variable with a pipe '|' and the name of the pipe function. Generally they're used to transform strings to match a pattern, but one of the more common built-in pipes, the async pipe, can be used to convert a promise or observable value into the resulting output, so pipes are very flexible with how they can convert data. Other common pipes inlcude currency and date filters, for formatting money and timestamps for internationalization.
